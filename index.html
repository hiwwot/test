Narrative

Narrative In this Studio we used generative coding and assembling techniques to form creative environments. With this classroom design we questioned, the evolving function of the classRoom while a pandamic unfolds. We begin this class at a time when the globe is debating the function of the classroom and the extent to which each institution, city, and nation has recourse to it. Hundreds of millions of children are participating in an entirely different type of schooling, one that is constantly constrained, social, technical, physical, and ethical challenges. What kind of space shall serve the needs of a classroom while considering these constiants.
